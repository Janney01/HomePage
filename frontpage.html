PHILIPPINES

A stunning archipelago of over 7,000 islands in Southeast Asia, renowned
for its diverse culture, friendly people, and breathtaking natural beauty.
Among its top tourist destinations are Boracay, famous for its pristine
white-sand beaches; Palawan's El Nido and Coron, known for crystal-clear
lagoons and limestone cliffs; and the Chocolate Hills in Bohol, a unique
geological wonder.
